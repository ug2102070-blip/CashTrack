📱 Overview

CashTrack is a personal finance tracker app designed for users in Bangladesh. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

CashTrack হলো বাংলাদেশের ব্যবহারকারীদের জন্য একটি ব্যক্তিগত আর্থিক ট্র্যাকার অ্যাপ। এই গোপনীয়তা নীতিতে আমরা কীভাবে আপনার তথ্য সংগ্রহ, ব্যবহার ও সুরক্ষিত রাখি তা ব্যাখ্যা করা হয়েছে।

📊 Information We Collect

Account Information:

  • Name and email address (when you sign in with Google)
  • Profile photo (if provided via Google Sign-In)

Financial Data (User-Entered):

  • Income and expense transactions
  • Budget and savings goal information
  • Account names and balances
  • Category preferences

Notification Data:

  • If you enable the auto-import feature, CashTrack reads financial notifications (e.g., from bKash, Nagad) using Android's Notification Listener Service to automatically detect transactions.
  • Only financial notification content is processed. Non-financial notifications are ignored and never stored.

Device Information:

  • Device model and OS version (for crash reporting and compatibility)
  • App version

Camera & Photos:

  • If you attach receipt photos to transactions, the photos are stored locally on your device and optionally in your cloud backup.

Biometric Data:

  • If you enable biometric lock (fingerprint/face), authentication is handled entirely by your device's secure hardware. CashTrack never accesses, stores, or transmits your biometric data.

🔐 How We Use Your Data

  • To display your financial information within the app
  • To provide budgeting insights and analytics
  • To sync your data across devices (when cloud backup is enabled)
  • To send you optional notifications (budget alerts, reminders)
  • To improve app performance and fix crashes

🛡️ We do NOT sell, share, or monetize your personal or financial data. Your data is yours.

☁️ Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: All your financial data is stored locally on your device using encrypted local databases (Hive).

Cloud Backup: If you enable cloud backup, your data is stored in Firebase (Google Cloud) under your authenticated account. Only you can access your data.

Security Measures:

  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
  • Firestore Security Rules restrict data access to the authenticated user only
  • Optional biometric/PIN lock for app access
  • Optional screenshot protection

🔔 Notification Access

CashTrack offers an optional feature to automatically detect financial transactions from notifications (bKash, Nagad, bank alerts, etc.).

  • This feature uses Android's Notification Listener Service
  • Only notifications matching financial keywords are processed
  • No notification data is sent to external servers
  • You can enable or disable this feature at any time in Settings

🤖 AI Features & Third-Party Processing

CashTrack offers an optional AI assistant powered by Google Gemini. When you use AI features, the app may send your financial summaries (such as income, expense totals, category breakdowns, and budget context) to Google's Gemini API for analysis.

We do not send personally identifiable information such as your full name, email address, or bank identifiers to Gemini unless you explicitly include them in the prompt.

We also rely on third-party services including Firebase, Google Analytics, and Google Cloud infrastructure to provide authentication, backups, crash reporting, and app analytics.

Cloud backup data is retained while your account remains active and until you delete your account or clear cloud data from the app.

📈 Analytics

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve the app experience. This includes:

  • Screen views and feature usage (anonymized)
  • App crashes and performance metrics

No personally identifiable financial data is included in analytics.

🗑️ Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:

  • Local data: Clear from Settings → Reset All Data, or uninstall the app
  • Cloud data: Delete your account from Settings → Delete Account

Upon account deletion, all your cloud-stored data is permanently removed.

👶 Children's Privacy

CashTrack is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
