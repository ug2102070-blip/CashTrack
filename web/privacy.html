Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

CashTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is a personal finance management application designed for users in Bangladesh. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google Sign-In or email, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Financial Data: Transactions, budgets, goals, debts, investments, and account balances that you manually enter into the app.
  • Voice Input: If you use the voice input feature, audio is processed using on-device speech-to-text and is not stored or transmitted.

Information collected automatically:

  • Notification Data (with permission): If you enable the auto-import feature, CashTrack uses Android's Notification Listener Service to read financial notifications from mobile financial services (bKash, Nagad, Rocket, bank alerts) to automatically detect and import transactions. Only financial notification content is processed. All data is processed locally on your device and is not uploaded to any server. Non-financial notifications are ignored and never stored.
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for analytics and crash reporting.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage data through Firebase Analytics to improve user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the CashTrack service
  • To sync your financial data across devices (when you enable cloud sync)
  • To provide AI-powered financial insights (using Google Gemini, with anonymized data)
  • To send you budget alerts, bill reminders, and debt notifications
  • To improve the app through anonymous analytics

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Your financial data is primarily stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (Hive database).
  • Cloud Sync: If enabled, data is synced to Google Firebase (Firestore), which uses encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Biometric Security: The app supports fingerprint/face authentication for additional security.
  • Screenshot Protection: Optional setting to prevent screenshots of sensitive financial information.
  • API Keys: Sensitive credentials are stored using Flutter Secure Storage with platform-level encryption.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal financial data with third parties. Data is only shared with:

  • Google Firebase: For authentication, cloud sync, and analytics (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Google Gemini AI: Anonymized financial summaries for AI insights (no personally identifiable information is sent)

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your data is retained as long as you use the app.
  • You can delete all local data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • To delete cloud-synced data, contact us at iamjahid.2025@gmail.com.
  • You can request complete account deletion through the app's Settings page.

7. Children's Privacy

CashTrack is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
